Research

I am investigating the impact that large storms have on their local environment. I am using laboratory experiments, numerical modelling and field data to investigate the role cold pools in the transmission of energy away from the storm. Specifically, using a lock release tank and high resolution stereoscopic PIV to analyse gravity currents in homogeneous and two layer fluid and the UK Met Office Large Eddy Model to perform idealised simulations in an atmospheric context.
